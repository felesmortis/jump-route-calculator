The Alliance Courier Service

This service is provided by members of the alliance using thier jump freighters. It exists to allow you to easily move items back and forth from trade hubs safely.

Contracting

You may want to create a character on your main account and keep it, with no skills, in a trade hub. You can then use this character to buy things from the market, and create contracts, as well as sell items you have shipped to the hubs. If it's on the same account, that character will cost you nothing at all, and will not need skill training. Another option is to fly to a trade hub in a shuttle to set them up, but survival of both your shuttle and your pod are not guarenteed.
